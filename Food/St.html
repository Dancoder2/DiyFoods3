Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spaghetti
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, garlic

How to Make:

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling water until soft.
  2. Heat tomato sauce in a pan with olive oil and garlic.
  3. Drain spaghetti and add to the sauce.
  4. Mix well, season, and serve with grated cheese.
