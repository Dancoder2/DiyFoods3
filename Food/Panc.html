Banana Oat Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • ½ cup oats
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ⅛ tsp salt

Steps:

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Add oats, egg, milk, baking powder, and salt; mix well.
  3. Heat a pan with a little oil over medium heat.
  4. Pour small amounts of batter into the pan to form pancakes.
  5. Cook for 2–3 minutes on one side until golden.
  6. Flip and cook the other side for 2 minutes.
  7. Serve with honey or fresh fruit.
