Baked Chicken and Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 potato (diced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

How to Make:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss chicken and diced potatoes in oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place on a baking tray.
  4. Bake for 25–30 minutes until chicken is golden and potatoes are crispy.
