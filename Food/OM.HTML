Oatmeal Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oats
  • 2 cups milk or water
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Fruits or nuts for topping

How to Make:

  1. Boil milk or water in a small pot.
  2. Add oats and stir continuously for 5 minutes.
  3. Add honey for sweetness.
  4. Serve in a bowl and top with fruits or nuts.
