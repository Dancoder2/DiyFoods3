Tuna Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 can tuna
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Lettuce and tomato slices

How to Make:

  1. Mix tuna and mayonnaise in a bowl.
  2. Spread the mixture on one bread slice.
  3. Add lettuce and tomato slices.
  4. Top with another bread slice and cut diagonally.
