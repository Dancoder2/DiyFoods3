Beef Tacos

Ingredients:

  • Taco shells
  • ½ lb ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Cheese, lettuce, tomato

How to Make:

  1. Cook beef in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and mix well.
  3. Spoon beef into taco shells.
  4. Add cheese, lettuce, and tomato.
