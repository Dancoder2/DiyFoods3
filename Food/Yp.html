Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup granola
  • ½ cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tbsp honey

How to Make:

  1. In a tall glass, layer yogurt, granola, and berries.
  2. Repeat the layers until the glass is filled.
  3. Drizzle honey on top.
  4. Chill for 5–10 minutes before serving.
