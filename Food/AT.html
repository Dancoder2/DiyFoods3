Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: tomato slices or boiled egg

How to Make:

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. Mash avocado in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spread avocado on the toast evenly.
  4. Add toppings like tomato or egg slices if desired.
