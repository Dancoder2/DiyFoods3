Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 grilled chicken breast
  • 2 cups lettuce
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes
  • ¼ cup shredded cheese
  • Salad dressing

How to Make:

  1. Grill the chicken breast and slice it into strips.
  2. Mix lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese in a bowl.
  3. Add the sliced chicken on top.
  4. Drizzle your favorite salad dressing before serving.
