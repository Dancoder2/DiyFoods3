Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup cheese
  • ¼ cup diced bell peppers and onions
  • 2 tbsp salsa
  • 1 tbsp butter

How to Make:

  1. Scramble the eggs with butter and veggies in a pan.
  2. Place the cooked eggs on a tortilla.
  3. Add cheese and salsa.
  4. Roll up the tortilla tightly.
  5. Heat it for 1 minute per side in the pan until golden.
